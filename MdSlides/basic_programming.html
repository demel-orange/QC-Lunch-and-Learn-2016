Basic Programming

Simulation

  • R makes simulating random numbers very easy. For example, from a standard normal:
rnorm(5)
## [1]  1.5220  0.3320 -0.9270  0.2638  1.1510
  • Or, say \(X \sim \mathcal{N}\left(12, 4\right)\):
x <- rnorm(1e+06, 12, 2)
mean(x)
## [1] 12
var(x)
## [1] 4.002
  • Note that R parameterizes the normal in terms of the standard deviation

PDF

-PDF of our example:

dnorm(10, 12, 2)
## [1] 0.121
dnorm(12, 12, 2)
## [1] 0.1995
dnorm(Inf, 12, 2)
## [1] 0

CDF and Quantile Function

  • CDF:
pnorm(10, 12, 2)
## [1] 0.1587
pnorm(12, 12, 2)
## [1] 0.5
pnorm(Inf, 12, 2)
## [1] 1
  • Quantile function:
qnorm(0.16, 12, 2)
## [1] 10.01
qnorm(0.5, 12, 2)
## [1] 12
qnorm(1, 12, 2)
## [1] Inf
